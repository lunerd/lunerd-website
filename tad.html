the athlete discovery

The Athlete Discovery is a business primarily focused on finding and teaching exceptionally talented, young basketball players but athletes in general. I was hired to design a crest or badge of sorts that would serve as a team/company badge for use on apparel including jerseys, shirts & caps.

Concepts

As with most illustrations of this style I started off sketching and drawing on paper. After getting a few concepts close to finished on paper I moved to vectorizing the illustrations. The client narrowed it down to their favorite designs and I proceeded to make them vectors. I included two short time lapse videos of the process.

Final Product

The final product ended up being sort of an amalgamation of the concepts, with a long shield look but also circular in the middle. I wanted to encapsulate the feeling a badge and crest but with a slight variation of all the more traditional looking badges.
