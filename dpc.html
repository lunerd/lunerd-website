DePasquale & Cole

DePasquale & Cole are a Downtown, Los Angeles law firm that has been in business for over 20 years. I was hired to completely redesign and develop their website. I provided design, development, and some photography for the project.

Phase 1

The first step of the project was research, including meeting with the partners, getting to know them and what they're daily flow was. This allowed me to put together a much more cohesive strategy as to what they needed from their website. This also included wireframing and taking photos of them at work. Both parties agreed the simpler more concise the website the better.

Phase 2

Once all the necessary materials were gathered, the development process could start. Wordpress was the best option for their price and timeline so I chose a template and edited to my and their liking using HTML, CSS, and some Javascript. I got my hands a bit dirty with some PHP as well and, it ended with the deployment of the site. The website is also fully responsive.
