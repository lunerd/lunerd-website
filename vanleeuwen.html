Van Leeuwen

Van Leeuwen Distribution is a wholesale dealer of after market motorcycle parts. I was an in-house designer for over a year and although my job consisted of many responsibilities, the main one was coding and re-designing the website including responsiveness.

Website

As per usual, the process was started with planning. The first step was wireframing and deciding what would be the best plan would be for the site, then I could write code accordingly. The second step was getting into the code and deciding what the best course of action would be for the site and its mobile layouts. I decided to use Twitter bootstrap for this project.
