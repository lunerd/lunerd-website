Creative Recruit

Creative Recruit is a company that helps emerging companies employ people that are a good fit for the company. They hired me to build a website and design a logo. The website has since been edited but the logo is still in use.

Design Concepts

Like any project of this type I always start out with rough sketch concepts that evolve both on paper and digitally. The company is serious about their work but they're also very flexible, adaptive, energetic, and young. I really wanted the logo to reflect the sophistication but also have a cool sleek feel.

Final Logo

The client felt the following logo reflected the company best. I wanted the pieces to stand alone but also work well in many environments. Whether its web, social, print including business cards, letterheads etc.
