laque'd

Laque'd is a high-end nail polish brand and I was tasked with creating a banner for their website and a social media promotion graphic for all their social channels. I wanted to incorporate traditional advertising design with the abstract idea of nail art such as patterns, lines, and shapes.

Process

The initial ideas were thought out and sketched out on paper. I wanted a significant blueprint to base my design off of once I transitioned into digital production. Once that phase was completed, digitizing the final banner was much easier and a much smoother process. The programs used were Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Lightroom.

Final

Based on the clients specs and needs, the product is the main focus of the design. That is the purpose of the lighter background contrasted by the dark nature of the products packaging. The banner design was primarily designed for use on a website but designed so it would also make sense printed or used on any social media posts. The squared format was specifically made for Instagram.
