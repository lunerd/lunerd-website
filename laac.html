As the in-house designer at the Los Angeles Athletic Club I was in charge of the promotional material for events held at and outside of the club. Projects ranged from posters, brochures & even web content management. So I chose a few specific projects that I thought encompassed my time there.

Event Posters

Club events were held very often for the membership and so I was to design posters for each occasion based on the theme. The three I chose were some of my favorite to work on. The first a squash exhibition match between two of the top squash players in the world, this was an event co-sponsored by Dunlop. The second a William Shakespeare inspired themed party which I got to do some custom lettering for, and the third a poster for French independence day.

Member Onboarding

The member onboarding brochure was used to educate new members of the club as to what they had access to and what they could enjoy at the Los Angeles Athletic Club. It was part of a comprehensive new member package that all new members recieved upon joining.
